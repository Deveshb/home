How do we do it?

Our AI ninja will take care of writing back-end code and front-end for you. So, you the only thing you need to worry about is what you're planning to build.

Never spend time coding the UI and forms?

No more. Well, the only thing which you need to do is drag and drop entities on to the canvas and our AI ninjas will take care of generating forms for you. We give you full control of the branding and the look-and-feel. Feel free to change them as need be.

You own your data.

You completely own your data, and can export it to whichever format you choose. We dont lock your data in our databases adn you can feel free to opt out as need be.

We do provide you with auto-indexing, blob support, search, scalability and reliability automatically - If you know what those mean. So, hosting with us will not be the a "bad" decision you'll make. We promise.

Works across all devices

Our AI Ninjas will build you a responsive app, which will work on any device large or small. iPad - yes! iPhones - yes! Android Phones - yes! and Desktops - you know it.

We even give you full control over branding, so if you dont like what our AI Ninjas have built for you, feel free to customize according to your taste. We let you git clone your repo and have your ninjas work on them.

Crazy simple logic designer.

Want to send an email? Save a form? Edit something? Upload a photo or even a video or even connect to a 3rd party API out there on the internet. No worries!

We've built an awesome logic designer that super-charges your development and lets you write as less code (or even no code) as possible.

You've already built your web-app and published it on the internet. But oh wait! You need a new feature to be implemented super fast. No problem!
